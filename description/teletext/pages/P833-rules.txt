Cancellation and refunds(6/7)         
                                        
                                        
 Tokens will be refunded in the         
 following cases: (2/2)                 
                                        
 * If any song is bid and fails to play 
   at all, tokens will be refunded for  
   the bid. * If Pinball chooses the    
   wrong table and gives a x0 payout,   
   any bets on that table will be       
   refunded. * If Pinball has achieved a
   score high enough to receive a       
   payout, but crashes or achieves a    
   lower score due to a glitch, tokens  
   will be issued for the higher score.
